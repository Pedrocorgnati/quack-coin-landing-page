## Brand Personality
- Playful but professional
- Friendly yet reliable
- Approachable, not intimidating
- Memorable without being frivolous

## Symbolism
The duck represents adaptability, prosperity, and community. QuackCoin embraces a lighthearted mascot while maintaining serious infrastructure and economic logic.

## Narrative
QuackCoin was born from the idea that finance does not need to be cold or complex. By blending humor, clarity, and real-world utility, the project creates an inclusive digital economy.

## Visual Direction
- Clean, modern, minimalist design
- Bright but controlled color palette
- Strong iconography and mascots
- UI focused on clarity and ease of use

## Tone of Voice
- Clear and transparent
- Slightly humorous
- Never misleading
- User-focused
