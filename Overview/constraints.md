## Legal & Compliance
- No promise of profit or appreciation
- Token classified strictly as a utility
- Clear disclaimers in UI and documentation
- No misleading marketing

## Economic Constraints
- No unlimited minting
- Daily reward caps
- Controlled APY
- Revenue-backed incentives only

## Technical Constraints
- Prefer battle-tested libraries (e.g. OpenZeppelin)
- Avoid unnecessary on-chain complexity
- Favor off-chain logic for iteration speed
- All critical rules must be documented

## Product Constraints
- Invite-only access
- Anti-bot and anti-abuse systems
- Transparent reward logic
- User-first design decisions

## Governance Constraints
- No unilateral rule changes without documentation
- Future governance must be opt-in and transparent
